# Questionnaire Patterns

## Stable Project Identity

- What should this workspace optimize for?
- What does good output look like?
- What examples should future stages imitate or avoid?

## Domain Rules

- Which facts, rules, formulas, policies, or constraints must remain stable?
- Which parts require deterministic scripts instead of model judgment?

## Stage-Specific References

- What examples should this stage follow?
- What mistakes should this stage avoid?
- What checks should happen before its output is accepted?

## Review And Ownership

- Who approves this stage?
- What must be visible at the review gate?
- When should the workflow stop rather than continue?
