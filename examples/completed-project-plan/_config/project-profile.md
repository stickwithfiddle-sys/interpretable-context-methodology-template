## Project Aim

Design a repeatable planning workflow that turns a rough initiative into a source-backed project plan, milestone calendar, decision log, and reviewable go/no-go package.

## Users And Stakeholders

- Primary users: project owners creating an initial plan.
- Secondary users: contributors who need clear responsibilities and dates.
- Reviewers or approvers: sponsor or maintainer approving scope and risk.
- People affected by errors: users waiting on the project outcome and contributors doing the work.

## Constraints

- Budget: keep the first plan lightweight and file-based.
- Timeline: useful for a two-to-six week initiative.
- Technical environment: markdown, git, local CLI checks, and normal coding-agent workflows.
- Legal, privacy, compliance, or safety constraints: keep private stakeholder notes out of public outputs.
- Tools or vendors to avoid: avoid project-management SaaS lock-in for the core workflow.

## Definition Of Done

The workspace is ready when the stage map, scaffold plan, setup questionnaire, and validation report are reviewed and accepted.
