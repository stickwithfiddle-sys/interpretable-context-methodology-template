# Discovery Report Review Rubric

Use this rubric when reviewing `output/discovery-report.md`.

## Required Sections

- Known Facts
- Assumptions
- Open Questions
- Candidate Workflow
- Candidate Layer 3 Material
- Candidate Layer 4 Artifacts
- Risks
- Discovery Recommendation

## Required Terms

- traceability
- source quality
- review gates
- Layer 3
- Layer 4

## Forbidden Terms

- TBD
- lorem ipsum
