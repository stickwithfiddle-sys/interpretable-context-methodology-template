# Setup Questionnaire

## Project Identity

1. What decision should the research brief support?
2. Who will read the final brief?
3. What deadline or meeting does the brief need to serve?
4. What source material already exists?

## Evidence Rules

1. Which source types count as primary evidence?
2. Which source types are supporting context only?
3. What makes a source too weak to use?
4. How should confidence be labeled?

## Brief Style

1. Should the final brief lead with recommendation, evidence summary, or open question?
2. How long should the brief be?
3. Should the brief include a source appendix?
4. How should uncertainty be phrased?

## Privacy And Compliance

1. Which notes or transcripts must stay private?
2. Which facts can be quoted directly?
3. Which facts should be summarized without attribution?
4. What terms or claims must be avoided?

## Review Preferences

1. Who approves source quality?
2. Who approves the final recommendation?
3. What should block the workflow?
4. What should move into the decision log for future runs?
