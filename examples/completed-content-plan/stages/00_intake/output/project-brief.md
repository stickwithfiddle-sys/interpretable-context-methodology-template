# Project Brief

## Project Name

Content Plan Example

## Desired Outcome

Create a project-specific ICM workspace for producing a weekly content plan from rough notes, source links, product priorities, and reviewer feedback.

## Starting Material

- Raw content ideas collected during the week
- Source links and short notes
- Product or project priorities for the week
- Previous content examples, when available

## Audience Or Users

- Primary user: a solo builder or small team planning weekly content
- Reviewer: the person who approves topics before drafting
- Final audience: readers who expect useful, accurate, sourced content

## Success Criteria

- The workflow separates raw inputs, theme selection, calendar planning, and review.
- Every planned content item can be traced back to a source, decision, or stated assumption.
- A human can approve the plan before drafting begins.
- Repeated editorial preferences can move into stable reference files.

## Constraints

- Time: fit a weekly planning rhythm
- Budget: use local markdown files and simple scripts first
- Tools: markdown, git, and coding-agent workflows
- Privacy or compliance: do not include private notes in public-ready outputs
- Must avoid: unsourced claims and hidden assumptions

## Natural Review Points

- After raw inputs are organized
- After weekly themes are selected
- Before the final content plan is accepted

## Open Questions

- Which channels should the first version support: blog, newsletter, social, or all three?
- Should the workflow score ideas quantitatively or keep prioritization qualitative?
- What minimum source quality should be required before an item can enter the plan?
