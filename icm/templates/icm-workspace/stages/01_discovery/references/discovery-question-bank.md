# Discovery Question Bank

## Domain

- What domain knowledge must the agent have every run?
- Which terms need stable definitions?
- Which rules are factual, legal, regulatory, mathematical, or brand-specific?

## Workflow

- What steps happen every time?
- Where does human judgment matter most?
- What output from one step becomes input to the next?
- Which steps are deterministic enough for scripts?

## Users And Reviewers

- Who approves the direction?
- Who catches errors?
- Who consumes the final output?
- What would make them distrust the system?

## Risks

- What errors would be expensive if found late?
- What assumptions could invalidate downstream work?
- What should never be delegated to model judgment?
